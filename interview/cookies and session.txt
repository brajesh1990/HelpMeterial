cookies are used to store small amounts of data on the client side,

while sessions are used to manage and store larger amounts of data on the server side.

---type of cokies
Lifespan: Cookies can have different lifetimes:

Session Cookies: These are temporary and are deleted when the browser is closed.
Persistent Cookies: These have an expiration date and are stored on the user's device until they expire or are manually deleted.
